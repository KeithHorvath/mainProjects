How It Started

When I was 12 I got a paper route and earned my first paycheck. The moment that happened, I realized if I did a really good job, I could get more money.

A year of hard work later, and I was making almost 20% of my dad's salary, and I had another revelation: I could make this a serious career.

Fast-forward 15 years and Diamond Couriers was officially born. Diamond is more than just the adult version of the paper boy, though we do deliver documents, too. I learned best practices for carefully transporting everything from fine china to liquids to frozen chicken, and that's why people come to Diamond any time they have courier needs. As a local company, I get to meet the people of the community, and develop trust with them. Now, I'm connecting my community one package at a time.

Thank you for choosing Diamond Couriers, and for helping me make my childhood dream come true.

Rob, Diamond Couriers Founder and CEO

Giving Back

With community being central to our mission, we decided to give back. We've partnered with dozens of local restaurants and grocers to collect their unused foods at no cost to them, and hold a weekly food drive every Saturday morning for those who need nutrition assistance.

This started small...but soon word spread, and two local chefs volunteered to take the restaurant leftovers to make fresh, warm meals.

Today we serve hundreds of people weekly, and are able to provide a warm meal and a large bag of groceries to each person at no cost. This is Diamond's way of thanking the community for helping make us the #1 courier service in town.
